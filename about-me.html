Hi there! My name is Hassan and welcome to my writing portfolio.

Writing is my passion. I've been studying, practicising and doing content writing jobs for about 5 years. My friend Anas Aldasouqu has helped me devleop as a writer by offering my writing jobs. So far, i've written for Al Kindi Internation Hospital, Jordan, Almaz Wearables(Michigan, USA) and extraprayers.com(A website run by millionaire entrepreneur Abdur Rahman). In addition, i've written two ebooks on mental health and physical health.

How I stay committed to learning

I like to think of myself as a lifelong learner. I used to spend hours and hours learning, then try to create simple projects using what I learned or work new techniques into existing projects.

While that was fun, I felt like it would be helpful to share what I was learning and most things about my journey with the world.

How I got started

I started small with passion, curiosity and unending commitment.I listened to world class teachers for many hours in a day. I practised the skills by putting my learning into action. And in this journey, i've the support of my friend Anas. Anas Aldasouqi is an entrepreneur from Michigan,USA who gave me all the writing jobs mentioned above. He is a great admirer of my work and genuinely believes in what i write.

The praise for my teachers, fellow learners.... and most of all from my brother, friend and supporter Mr. Anas Aldasouqi has made it possible to gradually realize my writing potential.
